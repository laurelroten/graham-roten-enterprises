About Graham Roten Enterprises

With over a decade of experience in the trucking and materials industry, Graham Roten Enterprises has established itself as the trusted hauling partner for contractors, builders, and homeowners throughout Boone and the High Country region.

We specialize in reliable transportation of construction materials, heavy equipment, and quality aggregates. As an expanding enterprise, we combine our core trucking expertise with comprehensive project support services. Our modern fleet and experienced team ensure your materials arrive safely and on schedule.

From small residential projects to large commercial developments, Graham Roten Enterprises is your trusted partner for trucking, materials delivery, and project logistics throughout the High Country.
