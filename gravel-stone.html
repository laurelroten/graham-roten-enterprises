Service Overview

We provide high-quality aggregates including gravel, crushed stone, sand, and fill dirt delivered directly to your job site. Our materials are sourced from trusted local quarries and delivered with precision timing to keep your project on schedule.

What We Provide

  • Premium graded gravel and crushed stone
  • Various aggregate sizes and types
  • Fill dirt and topsoil delivery
  • Accurate load sizing and measurements
  • Timely delivery scheduling
  • Competitive pricing on bulk orders

Service Areas

We deliver quality aggregates throughout Boone, Banner Elk, Blowing Rock, Valle Crucis, Sugar Mountain, and the entire High Country region of North Carolina.

Material Types & Applications

Our materials are perfect for driveways, walkways, construction projects, landscaping, and drainage applications. We work with you to determine the right type and quantity of material for your specific needs, ensuring optimal results and cost-effectiveness.
